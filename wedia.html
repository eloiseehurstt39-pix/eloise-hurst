About

What is Wedia?

Wedia is a French SaaS company that provides a cloud-based Digital Asset Management (DAM) platform designed for large enterprises and marketing teams who need to centralize, organize, and distribute large volumes of digital content across multiple channels and markets.

🏢

Company Type

Private SaaS company headquartered in Paris, France. Operates across Europe and globally with enterprise-grade cloud infrastructure.

🎯

Core Mission

Empower marketing and communications teams to efficiently manage the full lifecycle of digital assets — from creation to distribution and performance tracking.

🌍

Global Reach

Serves multinational brands across 30+ countries, supporting multilingual content, regional asset distribution, and localization workflows.

☁️

Cloud-Native

Fully cloud-based platform with high availability, scalable storage, and secure access management built for enterprise requirements.

Platform

Core Features & Capabilities

Wedia's platform covers the entire content lifecycle, from ingestion and storage to delivery and analytics.

📁

Digital Asset Management

Centralized repository for all digital assets with metadata tagging, advanced search, version control, and access permissions.

🔄

Content Distribution

Publish and distribute assets across websites, apps, social media, print, and partner portals from a single source of truth.

🤖

AI-Powered Tagging

Automatic metadata enrichment using AI and machine learning to tag images and videos, saving hours of manual work.

🎨

Brand Portal

Customizable brand portals where internal teams, agencies, and partners can access approved, on-brand assets instantly.

🔗

Integrations

Connects with CMS, PIM, CRM, and e-commerce platforms including Adobe, Salesforce, SAP, Drupal, and more via APIs and connectors.

📐

Image Transformation

Dynamic media delivery with on-the-fly image resizing, cropping, format conversion (WebP, AVIF), and responsive delivery via CDN.

🌐

Localization Support

Manage multilingual and multi-market content with locale-specific asset variants and regional distribution workflows.

📊

Analytics & Reporting

Track asset usage, download counts, engagement metrics, and content performance to inform marketing decisions.

🔐

Rights Management

Manage usage rights, licenses, and expiry dates for assets to ensure legal compliance across all distribution channels.
